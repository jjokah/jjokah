# AI Mastery Track | AIMASTER

Your complete, practical guide to understanding, using, and building with Artificial Intelligence, from first principles to advanced engineering.

---

## Who This Is For

This track is designed for anyone who wants to move beyond surface-level AI experimentation and develop genuine, lasting competence. Whether you are a business professional, creative, developer, or technical architect, this course meets you where you are and builds you up to where AI is going.

No prior AI experience required for Module 1. Modules 4 and 5 assume comfort with the concepts introduced in earlier modules.

---

## Skills You Will Learn

- Understand how AI models work and why they behave the way they do
- Craft effective prompts using professional frameworks (S.C.O.P.E., D.E.S., SCAMPER)
- Use AI for deep research, data analysis, and strategic brainstorming
- Generate professional text, images, audio, and video content with AI tools
- Identify and mitigate AI hallucinations, bias, and ethical risks
- Build and automate workflows using no-code, low-code, and API-based approaches
- Develop software faster using AI co-pilots and conversational development
- Understand Machine Learning fundamentals without needing a math background
- Implement Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and model fine-tuning
- Design and orchestrate multi-agent AI systems for production use
