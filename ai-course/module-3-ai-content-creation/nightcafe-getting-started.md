## What Is NightCafe?

NightCafe is a browser-based AI art platform available at creator.nightcafe.studio. You do not need to install anything, and you do not need any coding experience. Sign up with an email address and you can generate your first image within a minute.

What sets NightCafe apart from other AI image tools is its combination of three things:

- **A range of AI models in one place**: Rather than committing to a single model (as you would with Midjourney or Adobe Firefly), NightCafe gives you access to several models and lets you switch between them freely. This matters because different models have different strengths.
- **A built-in community**: Daily Challenges, public galleries, and voting are all part of the platform. Your work exists in a social context, not just a solo tool.
- **Accessibility first**: The interface is designed to get you to a result quickly. You can generate a first image with a basic prompt and no settings adjustments, then gradually explore more controls as you get comfortable.

NightCafe has been running since 2019 and has grown to tens of millions of creators. By 2025, the platform logged over 167 million total creations.

## The NightCafe Interface

When you open NightCafe Studio, the main workspace is the **Create page**. Here is what you will see:

### The Prompt Box

The large text field at the top is where you describe what you want to generate. Type a description in plain English. If you are stuck, click the **Shuffle button** (top right of the prompt box) to generate a random prompt suggestion. This is a useful way to see what kinds of descriptions work well.

### The Model Picker

Below or alongside the prompt box is a dropdown labelled **Model**. This is where you choose which AI model generates your image. Each model produces distinctly different results. For your first image, Nano Banana 2 is a good starting point: it is fast, free to use, and handles a wide range of subjects well.

### Basic vs. Advanced Settings

NightCafe shows a simple form by default. A toggle or button reveals **Advanced Settings**, which include:

- **Aspect ratio**: The shape of your image (landscape 16:9, portrait 9:16, square 1:1, etc.)
- **Number of images**: How many variations to generate at once
- **Negative prompt**: A list of things you do NOT want in the image (covered in detail in [NightCafe Prompting Guide](nightcafe-prompting-guide.md))

You do not need to touch advanced settings for your first few images. The defaults produce reasonable results.

### The Generate Button

When you are ready, click **Generate** (or **Create**). The image appears in a few seconds to a minute depending on the model you chose.

## Your First Generation: Step by Step

1. Go to **creator.nightcafe.studio** in your browser
2. Create a free account (email address only)
3. In the prompt box, type a description of what you want to see. Start simple:
   > *"A fox sitting in a snowy forest, golden hour light, photorealistic"*
4. In the model picker, select **Nano Banana 2**
5. Leave all other settings at their defaults
6. Click **Generate**
7. When the image appears, you can:
   - **Download** it to your device
   - **Publish** it to your public NightCafe gallery
   - **Like, Save, or Share** it within the platform
   - **Remix** it by adjusting the prompt and regenerating

That is your first image. The model processed your description and produced a result in seconds. If you do not like it, change the prompt slightly and generate again. Iteration is normal and expected.

## Understanding Credits

Credits are NightCafe's generation currency. Each time you generate an image, you spend a small number of credits. The cost depends on the model you use.

### How Credits Work

| Model tier | Approximate credit cost |
|---|---|
| Fast models (Nano Banana 2, Flux 2 Klein 4B Fast) | 0 credits (free on basic plan) or very low |
| Standard models | 1 credit per image |
| PRO models (Nano Banana Pro, Seedream 4.0) | Higher; requires PRO subscription |
| Video models | Higher than image models |

NightCafe gives you **free daily credits** just for being active on the platform. The exact amount varies, but daily logins and publishing creations keep a small credit balance refreshed.

### Free vs. Paid Tiers

| Feature | Free Account | PRO Subscription |
|---|---|---|
| Daily credits | Yes (limited) | Yes (more) |
| Access to standard models | Yes | Yes |
| Access to PRO models | No | Yes |
| Daily Challenges with PRO models | No | Yes |
| Fine-tuning (LoRA) | 1 free face model | Full access |
| Storage and history | Limited | Expanded |

PRO plans start at approximately $7.49 per month and unlock the most capable models, including Nano Banana Pro and Seedream 4.0. You can use NightCafe productively on a free account; the PRO models simply produce higher-quality or more specialized results.

## Earning Free Credits

You do not need to pay immediately to keep generating. NightCafe has several ways to earn credits without spending money.

### Daily Activity

Publishing your creations, participating in Daily Challenges, and maintaining a generation streak all earn small credit rewards. Many active community members sustain their creative practice entirely on earned credits.

### The Referral System

NightCafe has a referral program that rewards you every time someone you invite becomes active on the platform.

**How to find your referral link:**
1. Click your profile icon in the top-right corner
2. Select **Refer Friends** from the dropdown
3. Copy your unique referral link

**What happens when someone joins with your link:**
- They receive 20 free Pro Creations immediately
- You earn credits when they publish art for the first time
- You earn credits when they enter their first Daily Challenge
- You earn credits when they upgrade to a PRO plan
- If they stay PRO, you continue earning credits each time their subscription renews

The referral dashboard inside NightCafe shows a list of referred users and the credits you have earned from each milestone. This is an ongoing earning relationship, not a one-time reward.

**A practical approach:** Share your link naturally. Post an image you are proud of, mention that you made it on NightCafe, and include your referral link. People who are curious will sign up, and you earn credits from their activity without any pressure.

## Hands-On Practice

### Exercise 1: Your First Three Images

Generate three images using three different subjects. For each one:
1. Write a one-sentence prompt (subject + setting + one style word)
2. Use Nano Banana 2
3. After each generation, note what worked and what surprised you

Example subjects: a rainy city street, a fantasy library, a cat in a teacup. Compare the three images. What patterns do you notice in how the AI interprets descriptions?

---

### Exercise 2: Prompt Comparison

Take one subject and generate it twice: once with a short vague prompt and once with a longer specific prompt.

Short: *"A mountain landscape."*

Longer: *"A snow-capped mountain at dawn, reflected in a still alpine lake, soft pink and purple clouds above, photorealistic, wide shot."*

Compare the two results side by side. At what level of detail did the quality step-change most noticeably?

---

### Exercise 3: Model Comparison

Use the exact same prompt and generate it with two different models: Nano Banana 2 and one other model available in the picker (Seedream 4.0 or Flux 2 Klein 4B if available on your plan).

Evaluate: How do the visual styles differ? Which model better fits the subject you described? Which would you choose for future generations of that type?

---

### Exercise 4: Referral Setup

Set up your referral link today:
1. Find it in your profile dropdown under Refer Friends
2. Copy it and keep it somewhere accessible
3. Share it in one place where it fits naturally (a social post, a message to someone you know who is curious about AI art, or your bio)

How many referral milestones has it triggered after one week?
